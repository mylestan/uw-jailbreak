Director positions are out! We are always looking for passionate people to help make UW Jailbreak 2013 a reality. Below, we’ve listed current opportunities with UW Jailbreak. We’re a volunteer–run organization, so you’ll be compensated with smiles, hugs, and high–fives. The application deadline is Friday, April 19th. The general responsibilities for each position are outlined below:

Finance Director

Your responsibilities:

  • Handling our budget.
  • Maintaining our financial accounts.
  • Possibly working with online finance systems such as PayPal.
  • Preparing our financial documents.
  • Reaching out to and managing sponsors and partners.

We are looking for:

  • Current student at University of Waterloo.
  • Experience in similar finance work (volunteer, Internship).
  • Outgoing and well–spoken person who can make a first approach to potential sponsors.

Business Development Director

Your responsibilities:

  • Develop new sponsor and partner relationships.
  • Negotiate and follow through with potential sponsor and partnership agreements.
  • Work with existing partners to ensure that our vision and values for the event align.

We are looking for:

  • Current student at the University of Waterloo.
  • Experience in business, communicating with organizations
  • Experienced with gathering sponsorships for events/organizations
  • Someone with strong communication skills.

Legal Director

Your responsibilities:

  • Work on all legal documents, such as our sponsor and partnership agreements and liability/waiver forms.
  • Handling our liability requirements.
  • Providing general legal advice throughout the team.

What we are looking for:

  • (Prefer) Current student at University of Waterloo.
  • Effective written communication skills.
  • Experience working with legal material (volunteer, Internship, Academics).
  • Some knowledge of insurance, liability, and travel policies.

Technical Development Director (and team)

Note that we will be hiring a few people to tackle these roles. If you have experience in specific areas of what we need to do, get in touch — don’t worry fitting all the requirements. There will most likely be a team lead and then a few devs — tell us which you’re interested in.

Your responsibilities:

  • Building out the back–end of our webpage to handle blogging, registration, and tracking.
  • Iterating on our front–end design and managing the blog.
  • Building out our mobile tracking solution for the event (native, mobile web, SMS, API — we’re not sure yet).

What we are looking for:

  • Current student(s) at University of Waterloo.
  • Learn quickly and build iteratively.
  • Comfortable with front and back end work (especially Python, Git, and Heroku).
  • Comfortable with mobile app dev.
  • Facebook, Twitter, and Google Maps API knowledge is a plus.

Note: If you’re not qualified in all of these areas but still want to get involved, send us an email anyways!

Email us (at uwjailbreak@gmail.com) or send us a message on Facebook, including:

  • The position you are applying to.
  • Your general resume.
  • A paragraph or two on why this position is a great fit for you, and why you're a great fit for us!

Again, the deadline is Friday, April 19th!. Take a study break and get your application in!

If you don’t see a position you think is right for you but still want to get involved, please contact us anyways!
